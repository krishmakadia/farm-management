About FarmFlow

Welcome to FarmFlow, your comprehensive farm management solution. We are dedicated to helping farmers efficiently manage their farms by providing a platform that stores and tracks essential information about farms, soil types, crops grown, and water requirements.

Key Features

  • Farm Details Storage: Easily store and manage information about your farms, including size, location, and soil type.
  • Soil Type Management: Keep track of the different soil types used in your farms and their properties.
  • Crops Information: Record details about the crops grown, including planting dates, growth stages, and expected yields.
  • Water Requirements: Monitor and manage the water requirements for different crops to ensure optimal growth.
